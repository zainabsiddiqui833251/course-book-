# Specification: Humanoid Robots in 2025

**Feature Name:** Humanoid Robots: Deployments, Ethics, and Policy

**Created:** 2025-12-22

**Author:** Gemini CLI

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the specification for documenting current advancements, real-world applications, and critical discussions surrounding humanoid robots in 2025. It covers key robotic platforms, their deployment scenarios, and the profound economic, ethical, safety, and policy implications.

## 2. Goals

- To provide a comprehensive overview of the state-of-the-art in humanoid robotics as of 2025.
- To detail the practical applications and deployments of humanoid robots across various sectors.
- To explore the significant economic impacts, including debates on job displacement and creation.
- To address the ethical considerations, safety protocols, and alignment challenges associated with advanced physical AI.
- To summarize recent deployment videos and relevant policy discussions.

## 3. Scope

**In Scope:**
- Current state and capabilities of prominent humanoid robots in 2025 (e.g., Tesla Optimus Gen 2, Figure 02, Atlas).
- Real-world deployment examples in home, factory, healthcare, and space.
- Analysis of economic impact, including arguments on job displacement and creation.
- Discussions on ethical considerations, safety protocols, and AI alignment for physical robots.
- Descriptions of the latest 2025 deployment videos and summaries of relevant policy discussions.

**Out of Scope:**
- Detailed technical specifications or proprietary internal designs of the robots.
- Future projections beyond 2025 unless directly related to current trends.
- In-depth legal analysis of international regulations.

## 4. User Scenarios

1.  **Researcher**: Needs to understand the current capabilities and market presence of leading humanoid robots in 2025.
    *   *Acceptance*: Researcher can easily identify and locate information on Tesla Optimus Gen 2, Figure 02, and Atlas robots.
2.  **Policymaker**: Needs to grasp the societal impact, ethical considerations, and policy landscape surrounding humanoid robots.
    *   *Acceptance*: Policymaker can find clear summaries of ethical concerns, safety issues, economic debates, and policy discussions.
3.  **Educator/Student**: Needs to find recent examples of humanoid robot deployments and understand the ongoing discussions.
    *   *Acceptance*: Educator/Student can access descriptions of recent 2025 deployment videos and find relevant policy summaries.

## 5. Functional Requirements

1.  **Robot Showcase**: The specification shall detail current humanoid robots in 2025, including Tesla Optimus Gen 2, Figure 02, and Atlas.
2.  **Deployment Scenarios**: The specification shall describe real-world deployments of humanoid robots in home, factory, healthcare, and space sectors.
3.  **Economic Impact Analysis**: The specification shall present information on the economic impact of humanoid robots, including the debate around job displacement and creation.
4.  **Ethical and Safety Considerations**: The specification shall cover discussions on the ethics, safety, and alignment of physical AI (humanoid robots).
5.  **Multimedia and Policy Integration**: The specification shall include descriptions of the latest 2025 deployment videos and relevant policy discussions.

- Users can identify and find information about at least 3 prominent humanoid robots from 2025 within 1 minute.
