# Quick Start Guide: Humanoid Robots Documentation

This guide provides a quick overview of how to access and understand the documentation related to Humanoid Robots in 2025.

## 1. Accessing Key Information

- **Current Robot Models**: Refer to the `spec.md` under `specs/003-humanoid-robots-ethics/` for details on models like Tesla Optimus Gen 2, Figure 02, and Atlas.
- **Deployment Scenarios**: Explore the `data-model.md` for defined scenarios such as 'Factory', 'Healthcare', etc.
- **Ethical and Policy Discussions**: Dive into the `spec.md` for overviews on safety, alignment, and policy areas.

## 2. Understanding the Data Model

The `data-model.md` file outlines the structure of data related to humanoid robots, including entities like `Humanoid Robot`, `Deployment Scenario`, `Economic Impact Factor`, `Ethical Concern`, `Policy Area`, and `Video`. This model defines the relationships and attributes for comprehensive data representation.

## 3. Further Steps

- **Review `research.md`**: For insights into how policy, economic impact, and video descriptions were scoped.
- **Refer to `plan.md`**: For the detailed planning process and future steps.
- **Consult `spec.md`**: For the full feature specification.

This documentation aims to provide a clear, philosophical, and forward-looking perspective on humanoid robots in 2025.
